FAQ

What is VYMMA?
VYMMA is the Vancouver Youth Mathematical Modeling Association, a non-profit organization focused on teaching students how to apply mathematics to solve real-world problems through modeling.
Who can join VYMMA?
VYMMA is open to high school students passionate about mathematics and problem-solving.
What activities does VYMMA offer?
We offer group projects, design activities, workshops, and opportunities to collaborate on real-world mathematical modeling challenges.
How do I get involved?
You can join by contacting us through our website or signing up for one of our events.
What is mathematical modelling?
Mathematical modelling is the process of using mathematical principles to convert a real-life scenario into an abstract model. This allows for deeper analysis of the systems around us.
Why is mathematical modeling important?
Mathematical modeling bridges the gap between abstract mathematics and practical applications, enabling innovative solutions to complex problems.
Can I join VYMMA if I have no experience?
Of course! VYMMA encourages any new student who is curious to try mathematical modelling. It is an important skill and can allow for deeper understanding of the systems around us.
What are the benefits of mathematical modelling?
Mathematical modelling encourages critical thinking and opens up opportunities for STEM careers. Students also learn how to use computer softwares and tools for modelling.
