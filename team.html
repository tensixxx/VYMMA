Our Team

Meet our wonderful executives

Bruce

Bruce is a grade 11 student and the founder of the Vancouver Youth Mathematical Modeling Association (VYMMA). Bruce was inspired to create VYMMA to bridge the gap between theoretical math and practical problem-solving. He believes that mathematical modeling is a powerful tool to address complex challenges and create meaningful solutions. As a visionary leader, Bruce is committed to fostering a collaborative environment where students can explore their creativity and develop the skills needed to make a positive impact on the world through mathematics.

Cary

Cary is a grade 10 student with a passion in mathematics. He believes that pure mathematics is not enough to create an impact on the world, and believes mathematical modeling is the key step. He deeply believes simplifying models are key to understanding more crucial issues and enjoys analyzing them to find the root causes.

Rachel Shi

Rachel is a grade 11 student with strong interest in STEM. She wants to solve real world issues through mathematical modeling. At the same time, she is looking forward to learning more and contributing to the world with her knowledge. As a team member, Rachel enjoys solving problems with other people by brain storming.

Honorary Chairman

Lang Wu serves as the Honorary Chairman of the Vancouver Youth Mathematical Modeling Association (VYMMA). He is a distinguished professor from the University of British Columbia specializing in biostatistics, with research expertise in areas such as longitudinal data analysis, survival data analysis, and constrained inference. With a deep understanding of how mathematical models can address complex real-world problems, Lang Wu provides invaluable guidance and mentorship to the association.
